Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond?

Our support team typically responds within 2-4 hours during business hours. For WhatsApp inquiries, response time is even faster — often within 30 minutes.

Do you serve students outside Pakistan?

Absolutely! We have students in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, Europe, and many other countries. Our online classes are designed for global accessibility.

What languages do you support?

Our teachers are fluent in English, Urdu, Arabic, and Pashto. We can accommodate students in any of these languages for instruction.

Do you offer classes for adults?

Yes! We welcome students of all ages — from children as young as 4 to adults in their 70s. It's never too late to start learning the Quran.

Can I get a free trial before paying?

Yes, we offer a FREE 3-day trial class (2-3 sessions) for every new student. No credit card required. Just fill out the enrollment form and mention "Free Trial".

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal, credit/debit cards, bank transfer, EasyPaisa, and JazzCash (for Pakistani students). Monthly payments are processed securely.

Can I request a female teacher?

Absolutely. We have both male and female certified teachers. You can request a female instructor for yourself or your daughter during enrollment.

Can I reschedule a class?

Yes, we offer flexible rescheduling. Just inform your teacher at least 4 hours in advance, and we'll arrange a makeup class at no extra cost.
