Our Courses

Comprehensive Quranic education tailored for every level — from beginners to advanced scholars

Explore our meticulously designed courses that blend traditional Islamic pedagogy with modern teaching techniques. Each course is taught live, one-on-one, by certified Ijazah holders.

Noorani Qaida

Duration: 2-4 Months Classes: 3-5 per week Level: Absolute Beginner

Noorani Qaida is the foundational course for anyone beginning their Quranic journey. This systematic guide teaches Arabic alphabets, pronunciation (Makharij), and basic Tajweed rules. Students learn letter recognition, joining letters, vowel signs (Harakat), Tanween, Maddah, Leen, and practical exercises to build fluency. Our instructors use interactive whiteboards and repetition techniques to ensure mastery before moving to Quran reading.

What makes our Noorani Qaida unique is the personalized pace — each student progresses according to their ability. Children as young as 4 years old, as well as adult beginners, have successfully completed this course and transitioned to Quran reading with confidence. By the end, students will be able to read any Quranic word with correct pronunciation.

  • 29 Arabic letters & their proper exits (Makharij)
  • Short vowels (Fatha, Kasra, Damma) & Long vowels (Madd)
  • Sukoon, Tashdeed, and Tanween rules
  • Basic Tajweed principles for beginners
  • Weekly assessments and progress tracking

Quran Reading (Nazra)

Duration: 6-12 Months (Complete Quran) Classes: 3-5 per week Level: After Qaida

Once students master the Qaida, they proceed to Nazra Quran — reading the Holy Quran with proper pronunciation and basic Tajweed. Our teachers guide students page by page, ensuring smooth, fluent recitation. Each session includes Sabaq (new lesson), Sabaq Parhna (revision of previous lessons), and Daur (overall revision). Students typically complete the entire Quran within 6-12 months depending on frequency and effort.

This course emphasizes consistent practice and builds a strong relationship with the Quran. Parents receive monthly reports highlighting progress, areas of improvement, and teacher feedback. Upon completion, students receive a certificate and can confidently recite the Quran in prayers and daily life.

  • Complete Quran recitation with proper Makharij
  • Application of basic Tajweed rules in reading
  • Fluency and rhythm in recitation (Tarteel)
  • Memorization of important Surahs and Duas
  • Optional: Daily homework and revision plans

Tajweed Course

Duration: 4-8 Months Classes: 2-3 per week Level: Intermediate

Tajweed (literally "to beautify" or "to improve") is the science of reciting the Quran as it was revealed to Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). This course delves deep into the intricate rules of pronunciation, stopping, starting, and characteristics of Arabic letters. Students learn the theoretical rules and practice them practically on selected Quranic verses.

Topics covered include: Ghunnah (nasalization), Idgham (merging), Iqlab (conversion), Ikhfa (hiding), rules of Meem Sakinah, Laam Sakinah, Qalqalah, and the various types of Madd (prolongation). Our teachers are certified in Qira'at and provide individual feedback. By the end, students will recite with the beauty and precision of expert reciters.

  • Complete Tajweed rules with practical application
  • Characteristics (Sifaat) of Arabic letters
  • Rules of Raa, Laam, and Noon Qutni
  • Advanced Madd (prolongation) rules
  • Certificate of Tajweed proficiency

Hifz Program (Quran Memorization)

Duration: 2-5 Years (flexible) Classes: 5-6 per week Level: Advanced

Our Hifz Program is designed for students who aspire to memorize the entire Quran with precision and strong retention. We follow a proven methodology: new lesson (Sabaq) of ½ to 1 page daily, recent revision (Sabaq Parhna) of previous 5-7 pages, and old revision (Daur) of the last 5-10 Juz. This three-tier system ensures long-term memorization.

Each student is assigned a dedicated Hifz teacher who monitors progress, corrects Tajweed, and provides motivation. We offer both part-time and full-time tracks. Many of our students have completed Hifz within 2-3 years and gone on to lead Tarawih prayers. Parents receive weekly progress reports and recorded sessions for revision.

  • Structured memorization plan tailored to each student
  • Daily revision schedule to prevent forgetting
  • Tajweed-focused memorization
  • Mock tests and full Quran review sessions
  • Ijazah preparation for advanced students

Tafseer Course (Quranic Exegesis)

Duration: 6-12 Months per Juz Classes: 2 per week Level: Intermediate/Advanced

Tafseer is the profound study of understanding the meanings, context, and lessons of the Quran. This course covers selected Surahs and Juz, explaining the reasons for revelation (Asbab al-Nuzul), linguistic analysis, and practical applications in daily life. Students learn from classical Tafseer works like Tafseer Ibn Kathir and contemporary scholarship.

The course bridges the gap between recitation and comprehension, enabling students to connect deeply with Allah's words. We discuss historical events, rulings, parables, and spiritual insights. By the end, students gain a holistic understanding of the Quran's message and how to implement it in modern times.

  • Detailed explanation of selected Surahs (e.g., Yaseen, Rahman, Mulk, etc.)
  • Background of revelation and historical context
  • Linguistic miracles and rhetorical analysis
  • Contemporary application of Quranic teachings
  • Interactive Q&A and reflective discussions
