Privacy Policy

Your privacy and trust are our top priorities. Read how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Last Updated: March 15, 2026

At Sadeeq Online Academy, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, enroll in our courses, or interact with our services. Please read this policy carefully. By using our website and services, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our platform, including:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address (if provided), and date of birth (for age-appropriate course placement).
  • Educational Information: Quranic learning level, course preferences, progress reports, teacher feedback, and assessment results.
  • Payment Information: Billing details, payment method (processed securely via third-party payment gateways — we do not store full credit card details).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, and usage statistics collected via cookies and similar technologies.
  • Communication Data: Records of your correspondence with us, including emails, WhatsApp messages, phone calls, and chat transcripts.
  • Class Recordings: With your consent, we may record sessions for educational and revision purposes. These recordings are stored securely and accessed only by you and your teacher.

2. How We Collect Your Information

We collect information through the following methods:

  • Direct Interactions: When you fill out enrollment forms, contact us via email or phone, register for a free trial, or subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Automated Technologies: As you navigate our website, we may use cookies, web beacons, and analytics tools to collect technical data about your browsing behavior.
  • Third-Party Sources: We may receive information from payment processors (for transaction confirmation) or social media platforms (if you interact with our pages).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following legitimate purposes:

  • To Provide Our Services: Process enrollments, schedule classes, assign teachers, deliver course materials, and facilitate live Quranic instruction.
  • To Communicate: Send enrollment confirmations, class reminders, progress updates, payment receipts, and respond to your inquiries.
  • To Improve Our Platform: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience, develop new features, and optimize our teaching methodologies.
  • To Ensure Safety: Monitor for inappropriate behavior, prevent fraud, protect the security of our platform, and comply with legal obligations.
  • To Personalize Learning: Tailor course recommendations, adjust teaching pace based on progress, and provide customized revision plans.
  • Marketing Communications: With your explicit consent, we may send you promotional materials about new courses, discounts, or Islamic educational content. You may opt out at any time.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing is necessary for the performance of the enrollment contract with you.
  • Legitimate Interests: We have a legitimate interest in improving our services, preventing fraud, and communicating with you about your account.
  • Consent: For marketing communications and optional data collection (e.g., cookies), we rely on your explicit consent.
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who assist with payment processing, email delivery, hosting services, and analytics. These parties are contractually obligated to keep your data confidential and use it only for the services they provide.
  • Teachers and Academic Staff: Your assigned teachers have access to your name, learning level, and progress reports to facilitate effective instruction.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation, we may disclose your information to comply with legal processes.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity, subject to the same privacy commitments.
  • Protection of Rights: To enforce our Terms of Service, protect our rights, property, safety, or that of our users.

6. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS technology.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to personal data, and they are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with firewalls and regular security audits.
  • Payment Security: Payment transactions are processed through PCI-DSS compliant gateways; we do not store full payment card details.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we continuously update our security practices.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Specifically:

  • Student Records: Retained for 5 years after your last active enrollment to maintain academic history and provide references.
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years to comply with tax and financial regulations.
  • Communication Logs: Retained for 2 years to resolve disputes and improve customer service.
  • Class Recordings: Retained for 6 months after course completion, unless you request earlier deletion.

After the retention period, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal exceptions).
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously given consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at sadeeqonlineacademy1@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy Request". We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality (e.g., navigation, form submissions).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. By continuing to use our website without changing your settings, you consent to our use of cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. Our services are designed for students of all ages, including minors under 18. For students under 18:

  • Parental or guardian consent is required before enrollment.
  • We collect only the information necessary for educational purposes.
  • Parents/guardians have full access to their child's records and may request deletion or correction.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

Parents/guardians may review, update, or delete their child's information by contacting us directly.

11. International Data Transfers

Sadeeq Online Academy operates globally. Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence, including Pakistan and the United States (where our servers are located). These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses, to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

12. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, such as payment processors or social media platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
  • Sending an email notification to registered users (for significant changes).
  • Displaying a prominent notice on our website.

Your continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
