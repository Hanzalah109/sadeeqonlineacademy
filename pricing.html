Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Every new student gets a FREE 3-day trial (2-3 classes) to experience our teaching methodology and teacher compatibility before committing to any paid plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal, credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and EasyPaisa/JazzCash for Pakistani students. All payments are secure and processed monthly.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time with 7 days' notice. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

Do you have discounts for siblings?

Yes, we offer 15% discount for 2+ family members enrolling together. Contact our support team to apply the family discount.

Are these prices in USD?

Yes, prices are in US Dollars. We offer competitive local pricing for students in Pakistan, India, Bangladesh, and other South Asian countries — please contact us for regional rates.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, upon successful completion of any course, students receive a verified certificate from Sadeeq Online Academy recognizing their achievement.

What platform do you use for classes?

We use Zoom, Google Meet, or our dedicated virtual classroom. All sessions are live, one-on-one, and can be recorded for revision upon request.

What if I miss a class?

We offer flexible rescheduling. Just inform your teacher at least 4 hours in advance, and we'll arrange a makeup class at no extra cost.
